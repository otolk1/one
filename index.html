Pikachu

Pikachu is an Electric Pokémon which evolves from Pichu. It is vulnerable to Ground moves. Pikachu's strongest moveset is Thunder Shock & Wild Charge and it has a Max CP of 938. Pikachu evolves into Raichu.

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur (Normal) is a Grass & Poison Pokémon. It is vulnerable to Fire, Flying, Ice and Psychic moves. Bulbasaur's strongest moveset is Vine Whip & Power Whip and it has a Max CP of 1,115. Bulbasaur (Normal) evolves into Ivysaur (Normal).
